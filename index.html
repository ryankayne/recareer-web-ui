Let's do something better for our country.

ReCareer is a wholistic approach to combatting recidivism by pairing trained ex-prisoners to meaningful work.

ReInvesting in People

ReCareer

3 problems. 1 solution.

Recidivism

In the US, the most up to date data shows that for state prisoners, 83% returned to prison over nine years, and for Federal prisons, 40% returned for non-violent crime and 64% returned for violent crime. Our country's current process is not working. ReCareer offers a part of the solution.

Recruiting

The greatest issue facing business owners today is finding and keeping employees. ReCareer connects employers with providers to match skill-based labor to their respective industries. With Unemployment at an all-time low, ReCareer can ease the search for skilled candidates.

Restarting

ReCareer's focus on the individual allows our candidates to begin again, to start a new life with reenvisioned goals. Helping candidates find work is one aspect, but reconnecting them to community and a different social structure leads to greater life-satisfaction and lower chances of recidivism.
